SLD Nexus Guide

✨ Beginner's Guide ✨

Complex combo and essence calculations done in 3 steps!

1 Select Target Units

Find the unit you want to build in the codex list and click on it.

✔️ Selected cards light up.
✔️ Hidden units can be adjusted up to 8 using the [ − / + ] buttons.
✔️ Super Hidden (SH) units are limited to 1 per deck.

2 Check Daily Essence

Selecting Hidden/Super Hidden units automatically calculates your Total Essence (Korhal/Aiur/Zerus).

✔️ Check the [Essence N] badge on the right of each unit card.
✔️ Hidden units used as materials are tracked automatically.
✔️ Your goal points are displayed in the [Total Essence] panel!

3 Gather Materials & Check Lineage

The total base magic units required are reversely calculated and displayed in the Central Matrix.

✔️ Just collect the magic units shown in the center numbers.
✔️ Toggle the [Lineage] button to expand the full recipe tree.

💡 안내: Click the [☀️ Light / 🌙 Dark] button at the top for your preferred theme!
